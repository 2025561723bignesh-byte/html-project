Perfect Omelette

Details

  • Prep Time: 5 mins
  • Cook Time: 10 mins
  • Servings: 1

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Optional fillings (cheese, vegetables)

Instructions

  1. Whisk the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Melt the butter in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the pan.
  4. Cook until the edges are set, then add fillings.
  5. Fold the omelette in half and slide onto a plate.
